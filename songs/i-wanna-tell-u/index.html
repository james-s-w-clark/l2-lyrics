Translation / Study Notes
花 hua1 flower | HSK2 — 花褪去花瓣 = flowers undress their petals 褪去 tui4qu4 to shed / to undress | petals falling off 消散 xiao1san4 to dissolve / disperse | dreams dissolving into dreams 哼 heng1 to hum | casual, expressive — repeated twice 音符 yin1fu2 musical note | 写给你的音符 = notes written for you 藏不住 cang2bu4zhu4 can’t hide | verb + complement pattern: 藏 (hide) + 不住 (can’t hold) 躺 tang3 to lie down | HSK4 — 躺在你身边 = lying by your side 身边 shen1bian1 by one’s side | HSK3 — very common, natural phrase 睡得着 shui4de2zhao2 able to fall asleep | 怎么睡得着 = how can I sleep? (potential complement) 牵 qian1 to hold (hands) / to lead | 牵你不松手 = holding your hand and not letting go 松手 song1shou3 to let go | 不松手 = won’t let go ✨ 原来 yuan2lai2 it turns out / so this is | HSK4 — “so love can be simple” 简单 jian3dan1 simple | HSK3 — 爱可以很简单 = love can be very simple 需要 xu1yao4 to need | HSK3 — 不需要找什么答案 = no need to find an answer 答案 da2an4 answer | HSK4 没节奏 mei2 jie2zou4 no rhythm | 节奏 (rhythm) HSK5 — but a great word to know 心跳 xin1tiao4 heartbeat | 心 (heart) + 跳 (jump/beat) — poetic 舞步 wu3bu4 dance steps | 舞 (dance) + 步 (step)
