Translation / Study Notes
渡 du4 to ferry/save (Buddhist) | the key word — 渡人 = to save/enlighten someone ✨ 菩提 pu2ti2 bodhi/enlightenment | Buddhist term — 菩提不渡她 = enlightenment won’t save her 诵经 song4jing1 to chant sutras | 诵经声变沙哑 = the sutra chanting turned hoarse 沙哑 sha1ya3 hoarse/raspy | 声音沙哑 = hoarse voice 袈裟 jia1sha1 kasaya (monk’s robe) | 怎能脱下袈裟 = how can I remove my monk’s robe ✨ — give up being a monk for love 木鱼 mu4yu2 wooden fish (percussion) | 收起了木鱼 = putting away the wooden fish drum 晨钟 chen2zhong1 morning bell | 晨钟再敲几下 = the morning bell rings a few more times 佛祖 fo2zu3 the Buddha | 笑问佛祖啊 = laughing and asking the Buddha ✨ 年华 nian2hua2 years of one’s life | HSK5 — 乱了谁的年华 = whose life has been thrown into disarray
